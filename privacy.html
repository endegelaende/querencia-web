Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The short version

This website does not track you. No cookies, no analytics, no fingerprinting, no third-party requests. We don't know who you are and we don't want to.

Hosting

This website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages, a service provided by Cloudflare, Inc. (101 Townsend St, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA). When you visit this site, Cloudflare may collect:

  • Your IP address
  • Date and time of your request
  • The page you visited
  • Your browser's user agent string

This is standard web server logging that we have no access to. Cloudflare's privacy policy applies: Cloudflare Privacy Policy.

Cookies

This website sets no cookies. Not first-party, not third-party. None.

Analytics & Tracking

We use no analytics service — no Google Analytics, no Matomo, no Plausible, nothing. We have zero knowledge of visitor counts, demographics, or behavior.

Fonts

All fonts are self-hosted. No requests to Google Fonts, Adobe, or any other external font service.

External Links

This site links to external services (GitHub, COPR, Flathub). When you click these links, their respective privacy policies apply. We add rel="noopener" to all external links.

Container Image

The Querencia Linux container image (ghcr.io/endegelaende/querencia-linux) is hosted on GitHub Container Registry. Pulling the image is subject to GitHub's privacy policy.

Data we collect

None. This is a static website with no forms, no accounts, no comment system, no newsletter, and no server-side processing.

Your rights (GDPR)

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and port your personal data, as well as the right to object to processing. Since we don't collect or store any personal data, there is nothing to access, rectify, or erase.

If you have questions, you can reach the project via GitHub Issues.

Changes to this policy

If we ever add features that process personal data (which we don't plan to), this policy will be updated accordingly. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
